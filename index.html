HOW IT WORKS

Welcome to the Atlas Cosmographicae Ore Buyback Program! Here you can sell ores, gas, ice, and planetary products to the corp. We pay you for your goods (tip: compress first) and then place sell orders in a trade hub. The profits go into the corp to help pay for things like ships for fleet operations! The standard purchase rate is % Jita Buy Price.

How to sell your stuff to the corp:

  1. Your ore/items/ships should be located at any accessible station/structure between HQ in Sortet VI - Moon 5 - DED Logistic Support and Ellmay - SP Refinery - T2 Ore Refine (Corwan Academy).
  2. Select everything you want to sell > Right click > Create Contract.
  3. Select 'Item Exchange' and below that select 'My Corporation' > Press Next > Check your items are selected > Press Next again.
  4. Click the + button and find and click each item type you want to sell then write in the quantities of the items you're selling.
  5. The 'Total' is displayed in the center of the calculator page. Write the amount shown in the 'I will recieve' field of the contract setup window in-game
  6. Send a discord or Corp message to Erroneous II to clear the contract quickly since EVE doesn't notify on corporation contracts.
